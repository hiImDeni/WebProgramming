Brief History of Karate

Introduction

Karate is a fighting art that evolved on the Japanese i slands of Okinawa. It was introduced to mainland Japan in the early 1900s and since the 1950s it has spread throughout the world. Kara-te literally translated means “empty hand fighting”. As the name describes, when practising karate you are fighting empty-handed i.e. without any weapons.

Karate in the Olympics

Karate will make its debut appearance at the Summer Olympics in 2020 in Tokyo, Japan. Olympic karate will feature two types of events, Kumite and Kata. Sixty competitors from around the world will compete in the Kumite competition and twenty will compete in the Kata competition.
